Project Title: Different Level Gaming Logo (2015)

I had to design a logo for a gaming website called Different Level Gaming. Its main shape consists of the letters DLG, presented as a 3D cube. This could be viewed as a reference to 3D gaming.

The logo also has an important element - a small heart on the top, which in most games is a common symbol for a new level. Another great advantage is that the heart can be given a different color for different teams.
