About


my story

You know how it happens... it always starts with Paint and Windows Movie Maker :) But since curiosity pushed me forward, I continued learning Photoshop for over 3 years. Meanwhile I also gained some experience in using different video editing software.

In 2009 I started practicing my skills at the first website for movie fan-art in Bulgaria. For over 5 years, I was able to improve my abilities there by designing movie covers, posters and trailers.

I've been a freelancer ever since and I've worked on a wide range of projects. I always try to push myself forward by improving my creativity and learning new things every day. Hey, you can never know everything!
