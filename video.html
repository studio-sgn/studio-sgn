Project Title: The Black Love Blueprint (2019)

Editing an entire full-length documentary film is not just very time consuming, but also requires a lot of energy and commitment. Such big projects can be a real proof for someone's professional skills.

Along with the video editing process, I had to come up with a design concept to be used as a theme throughout the film. Another part of the job was to create all motion graphics that were needed - title cards, end credits, lower thirds, visual animations, etc. I also had to create several trailers for the film - each one having a different time duration.

The Black Love Blueprint is available for streaming on Amazon Prime and Vimeo On Demand.

Project Title: How we make The Golden Apple (2018)

The Golden Apple is a fantasy animated series, based on Eastern European mythology, legends and folklore. The production company behind it is called Studio Zmei, which is an international cloud-based animation studio.

They were planning to create a series of behind the scenes videos demonstrating the different stages of pre-production, production and post-production in the animation process.

I had to come up with a simple design for the intro & outro parts that matches their visual style. I also had to create a few b-roll animations whenever was needed. Some other work challenges included delivering the videos within a given deadline and having to cooperate with other teammates.

Project Title: Rewinding Black (2016)

"Remembering The Stories We Should Never Forget" is the headline of this mini-series. Each episode presents the story of an important person in the history of African Americans. I ended up editing only two videos, since the project was later cancelled and no more episodes were being produced.

Project Title: Community Center TV Ads (2019)

The New Settlement Community Center (based in The Bronx, NY) offers their members a range of programs in health and nutrition, education, youth development and the arts. They needed a series of TV ads in order to promote some of their programs.

Editing the ads was an interesting but also a challenging process. The main goal was to keep all videos visually consistent and at the same time each one had to feel unique. This was done by choosing different background music, so that the tracks could match the mood of each video individually.

Another tricky part was the time limit - the shorter a video is, the harder it is to edit. Trying to fit as much useful information in only 30 seconds can be quite difficult. At the same time, the ads should make sense and not be just random phrases combined together.
