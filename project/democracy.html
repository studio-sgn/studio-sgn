Democracy: A Black American Horror Story


when
June 2017 - Jan 2018

who
Boyce Watkins Films

what
Film Editing • Motion Graphics

about
The film "Democracy: A Black American Horror Story" analyzes every area from politics to economics, to produce a groundbreaking, fearless dissection of black America, American politics and the future of the African American community.
The video editing process is the main focus of the project, but creating all motion graphics was also an important part of it. The first step was to come up with а design concept which was going to be used as a theme throughout the entire film. The final part of the project was editing a trailer for the film.
